Why Local SEO Matters for Solicitors

Most people looking for legal help search with local intent. Phrases like "family law solicitor Dublin" or "conveyancing solicitor Cork" are typed into Google thousands of times every month. If your firm isn't visible in those results, someone else's is.

The Google Map Pack

When someone searches for a solicitor in their area, Google shows a map with three local results at the top of the page. This is called the "map pack," and it gets more clicks than any other section of the search results.

Appearing in the map pack means your firm's name, address, phone number, reviews, and opening hours are front and centre. For solicitors in Dublin, Cork, Galway, Limerick, and Waterford, this is where most new client enquiries begin.

Getting into the map pack is not random. It requires a well-optimised Google Business Profile, consistent local citations, strong reviews, and relevant local content on your website.

Local Citations and Legal Directories

Consistent citations across trusted directories tell Google your firm is legitimate and established. We build and maintain your listings on the directories that matter most for Irish solicitors.

What Are Local Citations?

A local citation is any online mention of your firm's name, address, and phone number (NAP). Google uses these citations to verify your business information and determine your local ranking.

Inconsistent citations, such as an old address on one directory and a new one on another, confuse Google and hurt your rankings. We audit every citation, fix inconsistencies, and build new listings on relevant platforms.

Our citation work is backed by the expertise of Juris Digital, bringing years of experience in legal directory management to every engagement.

Key Directories for Irish Solicitors

  • Law Society of Ireland Find a Solicitor directory
  • Golden Pages and local Irish business directories
  • Google Business Profile
  • Bing Places for Business
  • LSRA (Legal Services Regulatory Authority) register
  • Irish legal directories and referral networks
  • Yelp Ireland and industry-specific platforms
  • Local chamber of commerce listings
Consistency is everything. Your firm's name, address, and phone number must be identical across every directory. Even small differences, like "Street" vs "St." can create problems. We handle all of this for you.

Frequently Asked Questions About Local SEO for Solicitors

What is local SEO and how is it different from regular SEO? ▼
Regular SEO focuses on ranking your website in organic search results nationally. Local SEO focuses on ranking your firm in location-based searches, particularly in Google's map pack. For solicitors, local SEO is often more valuable because most clients search for a solicitor in their area. The tactics are different too: local SEO relies heavily on your Google Business Profile, local citations, reviews, and location-specific content on your website.
How long does it take to rank in the Google map pack? ▼
Most solicitors start seeing improvements in map pack visibility within 2 to 4 months. However, highly competitive areas like personal injury in Dublin may take longer. The timeline depends on your current online presence, the strength of your competitors, and how quickly we can build citations and reviews. Firms that already have a verified Google Business Profile and some existing reviews will typically see faster results.
Do I need a physical office to rank in local search? ▼
Yes. Google requires a real physical address for a Google Business Profile listing. Virtual offices and PO boxes do not qualify. If you serve clients at their location or in court rather than from a fixed office, you can set up a service-area business profile instead. This still allows you to appear in local results for the areas you serve, but you won't display a physical address on your listing. We can advise on the best setup for your situation.
Can I rank in the map pack for multiple locations? ▼
If your firm has physical offices in multiple locations, yes. Each office can have its own Google Business Profile, and we can optimise each one to rank in its respective area. For example, if you have offices in Dublin and Galway, we would create separate profiles and location pages for each. You cannot, however, create profiles for locations where you do not have a real office. Google is strict about this and will remove fake listings.
Are client reviews compliant with LSRA advertising rules? ▼
The LSRA (Legal Services Regulatory Authority) has guidelines around advertising and testimonials for solicitors in Ireland. Client reviews on Google are generally acceptable as they are posted by clients voluntarily on a third-party platform. However, you should not incentivise reviews or publish misleading testimonials. We ensure all review strategies comply with current LSRA guidelines and help you respond to reviews in a professional, compliant manner.
How do you track the results of local SEO? ▼
We track map pack rankings for your target keywords across each location you serve. We also monitor Google Business Profile insights including profile views, direction requests, phone calls, and website clicks. You receive a monthly report showing exactly how your local visibility is changing, how many actions people took on your profile, and how your review count and rating are progressing. Everything is measured against your competitors so you can see where you stand.
