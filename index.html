LSRA Compliance & Legal Marketing Regulations

SEO for solicitors must comply with Irish advertising regulations. We build compliance into every strategy from the start.

Understanding the Rules

The Legal Services Regulatory Authority (LSRA) regulates how solicitors can advertise in Ireland under the Legal Services Regulation Act 2015. These rules restrict certain types of claims, particularly around personal injury work, and require that all advertising is truthful and not misleading.

Many solicitors avoid digital marketing because they are unsure what is permitted. This creates an opportunity for firms willing to invest in compliant SEO. You can absolutely market your practice effectively within the rules.

GDPR & Data Protection

Solicitor websites must comply with GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. This includes proper cookie consent, privacy policies, data processing agreements, and secure handling of client information submitted through contact forms. The Data Protection Commission takes enforcement seriously.

Key Compliance Areas We Cover

  • LSRA advertising restrictions for personal injury solicitors
  • Prohibited phrases and claims in legal advertising
  • Requirements for truthful, non-misleading content
  • Proper disclaimers and disclosures on solicitor websites
  • GDPR-compliant contact forms and data collection
  • Cookie consent implementation that meets Irish standards
  • Privacy policy requirements for law firm websites
  • Review and testimonial compliance considerations
Our compliance advantage: Every piece of content we create, every page we optimise, and every strategy we implement is reviewed for LSRA compliance. You focus on practising law. We handle the marketing regulations.
